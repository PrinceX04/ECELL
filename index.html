Empowering Ideas, Igniting Innovation

about us

Welcome to the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIIT Bhubaneswar – a dynamic hub of innovation, creativity, and transformation.

Our mission is to cultivate a culture of innovation, risk-taking, and business acumen. We strive to provide students with the necessary resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to help them embark on their entrepreneurial journey with confidence.

Learn More

Cultivating Innovators, Nurturing Entrepreneurs

Vision

At IIIT Bhubaneswar's Entrepreneurship Cell, we envision igniting transformative change by nurturing a culture of daring innovation. Our aim is to empower aspiring entrepreneurs with the skills and resources for impactful, sustainable contributions to society.
